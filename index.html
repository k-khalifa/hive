The Beehive Website Process

How we efficiently help bring our clients visions to life!

1

Show us 1-3 designs you wish to emulate

We will use these as inspiration and create a preliminary unique and powerful design to your specifications. Once you are happy with the look we move forward to the 2nd phase of the project!

2

Design Approval

Once you approve the design we will match this aesthetic within all pages and begin the development process. If you will be providing your own content for the website this typically signifies the time to begin putting it together for us so we can integrate it during the website build out.

3

Website Development

We will convert all of our designs into code and begin bringing your vision to life! We will also be integrating all content into all given pages, products and posts.

4

Speed Testing & Fine Tuning

Once all coding is completed and all content is integrated this is where we perfect website load times, check all links and ensure everything is running smoothly. If there are any additional changes needed before launch we tackle these and check all systems to ensure we are good to go.

5

Website Completion & Launch

On confirmation and approval from our client that everything is running to their absolute satisfaction we configure hosting, security & malware protection and launch the website!
